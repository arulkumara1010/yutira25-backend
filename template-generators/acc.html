Accommodation Details

Hello participants,
Upon request from many participants, we are glad to arrange accommodation for all the students who are in need of accommodation.

Rooms are available from the night of 23rd March 2023 to the 26th March 2023 evening (no accommodation will be provided on the 26th of March night).

Stay Details

Based on students' interests, we have given you some options for you to choose your preferred accommodation.

Boys

1. Common Hall
  • The students will be accommodated in a single common hall - dormitory together
  • Only limited seats are available (First come first serve basis)
  • No fee is required
2. Common Bathroom
  • Two / Four sharing rooms
  • The students will have to share a common bathroom
  • A cost of Rs. 150 must be paid per head per day

Girls

1. Common Hall
  • The students will be accommodated in a single common hall together
  • Only limited seats are available (First come first serve basis)
  • No fee is required
2. Common Bathroom
  • The students will be provided with rooms (4 / 6 sharing) and have to share a common bathroom
  • A cost of Rs. 150 must be paid per head per day
3. Attached Bathroom
  • The students will be provided with an attached bathroom in their room (2 sharing)
  • A cost of Rs. 600 must be paid per head per day

* For Free Accommodation
Common hall for both boys and girls is provided on a first come first serve basis due to limited accommodation access.
