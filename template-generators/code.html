Kriya 2023 - Code of Conduct

Greetings,
Please find below the Code of Conduct for KRIYA 2023. All participants are to adhere to the code of conduct, any form of misconduct will not be tolerated. Kindly read the code of conduct.

Behavior
a) All participants are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful and courteous manner. Any behavior that is deemed inappropriate, aggressive or offensive towards other participants, event convenors, faculty, stall representatives and Kriya volunteers will not be tolerated by the organizers.

b) Unnecessary roaming on campus is frowned upon. The schedule and campus map will be sent via email to participants.

c)Any form of cyber hacks into payment or databases will be severely punished and reported.
Prohibited Items
The following items are strictly prohibited at the event: firearms, fireworks, weapons, drugs, alcohol, and any other illegal or hazardous materials. Any violation of this rule will result in expulsion from the event.
Food Policy
There are 10+ food stalls at KRIYA 2023 arranged for the benefit of the participants. The stalls take both cash and UPI payments. Participants are to have their food only near the stall areas. It is prohibited to take the food inside the event venues.
Use of Technology
All participants are expected to use technology and equipment provided at the event in a responsible and appropriate manner. Any misuse of technology will result in immediate disqualification.
Intellectual Property
All projects and presentations at the event must be original and the intellectual property of the participants. Any violation of intellectual property rights will result in immediate disqualification.
Rights to Photography and Videography
Photography and videography are permitted at the event, but participants must obtain consent from all individuals being photographed or recorded.
Disqualification
Any violation of the rules and regulations may result in immediate disqualification from the event. The decision of the event organizers is final. Respective college authorities of the disqualified participant will be notified.
Belongings
Participants are to take care of all their belongings throughout the three days. The organizers or the institution bears no responsibility over loss or damage to your belongings.
Damage to Property
Any damages to the college or hostel property would lead to the participant paying a fine. Kindly ensure to cause no damages to any college property.
Best Wishes,
Kriya Team,
