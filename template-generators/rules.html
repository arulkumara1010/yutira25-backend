Kriya 2023 - Prepare yourselves!

Greetings,
We are excited to welcome you to KRIYA 2023! We have put together a list of rules and regulations to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all participants. Please take the time to read and understand these rules before the event.

Timings ID Cards
Kriya Registration Kiosks will be open from 8 AM onwards, on all three days, to collect your Kriya Kits and for on-spot registrations for events and workshops. All participants must carry your valid college ID card at all times. This is required for registration and entry into the event. Failure to produce a valid ID card may result in denial of entry. Your college ID card is necessary for entry into the college premises.
Inauguration Dress Code
Scheduled on 24th March 2023 from 9 AM - 9:30 AM at D-Block Conference Hall (Ground Floor). Kindly be seated 10 minutes before. Participants are required to dress appropriately for the event. We advise formals for men and kurta with dupatta for women.
Kriya Kit Event Requirements
Your KRIYA 2023 kit bag and ID wrist Tag will be issued at the Kriya Registration Kiosk. Kindly ensure to collect your kit bag. Ensure the wrist tag is on you at all times. You will be denied entry into events otherwise. Please ensure that you have all the necessary materials for your events or workshop. This will be specified in the event/workshop description in the website . We will provide access to the necessary technology and equipment, but please make sure to bring any additional materials you may need.

On-Spot registration Team Registration
Participants may also register on the spot for events. However, please note that availability of spots is limited for some events and workshops and we encourage you to register in advance to avoid disappointment. Team registrations are done on the spot, however individual participants are required to register themselves on the website prior to that. Teams can only be formed with the students of the same college.

Pro Show Certificates
The pro show is scheduled for 25th March 2023 at 5:30 PM. You will be required to show your KRIYA Wrist tags to enter. Participants who have paid the general registration fee are allowed to enter. Certificates must be collected at the end of the day in person, at the time specified by the organizers of the event. Certificates will not be issued through courier or other modes. The winners and runners of the event may have to stay for prize distribution, which will be intimated to them by the organizers of the event.
On-Duty for PSG Tech Students On-Duty for other Students
On duty will be provided to those who attend the events (attendance in the events will be noted). OD will be provided day wise with Forenoon/Afternoon specification. PSG College of Technology does not hold any authority over providing on duty for other college students. Certificate of Participation or the payment receipt emails may be used to avail on duty at your respective institutions.
